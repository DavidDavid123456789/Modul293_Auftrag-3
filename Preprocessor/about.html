About Dave's Burger

Dave's Burger is a small fast food restaurant with a big passion for good food. We make fresh burgers, crispy fries and tasty hot dogs every day. Our goal is simple: good food, fast service and happy customers.

We use fresh ingredients and special homemade sauces. Dave's Burger is the perfect place if you love burgers and fast food.
